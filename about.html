About me!

Who am I? My name is Kristine, but everyone calls me Smuller. I thrive on experiencing unforgettable moments. My biggest passion is attending concerts, where I find pure joy.

By day, I work as a software developer, but in my spare time, I embark on adventures to witness incredible performances. Engaging with artists, witnessing their talent firsthand, and immersing myself in the beauty of music is a source of pure bliss.

While I mostly listen to pop music, my taste in genres is diverse and encompasses a wide range of musical styles.
